About Us

About Us

Welcome to Urban GatAway Apartment, your beachfront haven in Sarande. Our 1-bedroom apartment offers a cozy retreat with modern amenities. Enjoy breathtaking sea views from the balcony and unwind in a space equipped with a cable flat-screen TV, washing machine, and fully equipped kitchen. Stay connected with free WiFi, and relax in comfort with air conditioning in every room. Our smoke-free environment and convenient shower facilities ensure a pleasant stay. With free on-site parking available, exploring nearby attractions like Sarande Main Beach is easy from our convenient location.
